Every day, all across the country, cats face threats that put their lives in grave danger. But in large part because of dedicated cat lovers who donate to Alley Cat Allies, we’re able to spring into action whenever we learn of a danger to cats. With your generous support, we can protect and save the lives of cats and kittens. Your generosity means we can be there for cats and kittens in need; providing humane care in disaster situations, transforming shelters to save more cats, and advocating for cat-friendly policies.
