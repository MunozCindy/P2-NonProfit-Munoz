Who We Are

What is Alley Cats Allies?

We are a resource for tens of thousands of dedicated cat caregivers, advocates, nonprofit groups, and volunteers driving change and accelerating protection for millions of feral and stray cats and kittens worldwide.

When it comes to cat advocacy, Alley Cat Allies is working in communities to champion low-cost spay and neuter policies and programs, as well as lifesaving Trap-Neuter-Return (TNR) and Shelter-Neuter-Return (SNR).

How We Work

We have more than half a million supporters across the nation and around the world.

Together with our team of cat experts, grassroots organizers, attorneys, and researchers, Alley Cat Allies pursues three main goals:

  • Reforming Public Policies About Cats
  • Saving the Lives of Cats in Shelters
  • Changing Attitudes Toward Feral and Stray Cats and Kittens
